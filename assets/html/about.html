About Me

I'm a

Hello! My name is Juan Simón Ibarra Podhajecki, also known as ElCalvoYT. I'm a 16-year-old student currently based in Germany, where I indulge my passion for all things computer-related. From gaming to programming, my journey with technology has been both exciting and rewarding.

In the past three years, I've immersed myself in various programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Python. I still vividly remember the day I created my first webpage on September 16, 2021. It was a project exploring how big businesses handle user data, accompanied by a symbolic game of Snake to illustrate the concept.

My skills progressed as I delved into PHP and MySQL, culminating in a simple yet functional webpage where users could log in, manage their accounts, and upload content for others to see—a project that marked the beginning of my entrepreneurial journey with Xinitry.

Under Xinitry, I offer services in web development and video editing, two passions that have fueled my creative endeavors. I run my own YouTube channel, where I showcase my editing skills and share various content, reflecting the growth and dedication I've poured into each video since its inception in 2020.

Beyond YouTube, I've ventured into live streaming on Twitch, where I've garnered a supportive community appreciative of my content. Additionally, I undertook the challenge of developing an ERP system tailored to my mother's small business, Rincón Poupée, streamlining her operations and integrating various APIs, including DHL, to enhance efficiency.

My thirst for knowledge led me to explore Linux and set up a local network server, where I host the ERP system. Currently, I'm delving into the intricacies of AWS services, expanding my skill set and knowledge base.

Outside of programming and video editing, I've ventured into the realm of visual effects using Blender, continuously pushing my creative boundaries. Moreover, I've produced several short video projects for school, covering diverse topics such as: Bitcoin Horror narratives The implications of AI on trust including a documentary film.
